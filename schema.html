üßæ Invoice, Refund & Payment Relationship Rules

  • ‚úÖ Use FACT_INVOICES when a Booking has multiple invoices (1:n)
  • ‚úÖ Use FACT_REFUNDS when a Booking has multiple refunds (1:n)
  • ‚úÖ Use FACT_PAYMENTS when a Booking has multiple payments (1:n)
